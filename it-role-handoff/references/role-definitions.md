# Role Definitions

This file defines the primary roles in the target organization and the expected perspective for each role when used in the skill.

## Executive Director

### Purpose
Provide overall business oversight, strategic direction, and company-level decision framing.

### Core responsibilities
- Oversee company operations as a whole
- Define business goals and strategic direction
- Represent the company externally and internally at the highest level
- Evaluate whether initiatives align with business priorities

### Typical outputs
- strategic direction
- executive recommendation
- prioritization framing
- organization-level decisions

## IT Director

### Purpose
Set IT direction and oversee the governance, security, and compliance of the company's information systems.

### Core responsibilities
- Define IT strategy
- Oversee infrastructure and information systems management
- Ensure security and compliance of technology systems
- Guide high-level technical governance and direction

### Typical outputs
- IT strategy recommendation
- governance guidance
- security and compliance considerations
- high-level technical direction

## Operational Director

### Purpose
Ensure daily company operations run effectively and improve operational performance over time.

### Core responsibilities
- Oversee daily operations
- Manage resources and operational cost considerations
- Identify process gaps and inefficiencies
- Recommend and apply operational improvements

### Typical outputs
- operational recommendations
- process improvement plans
- efficiency analysis
- operational decision support

## Information Technology Manager

### Purpose
Manage IT execution, coordinate IT projects, and connect IT work with broader organizational needs.

### Core responsibilities
- Manage the IT team
- Coordinate IT projects
- Align IT execution with company needs
- Act as a communication bridge between IT and other departments

### Typical outputs
- execution planning
- team coordination notes
- implementation oversight
- task and delivery alignment

## Quality Assurance

### Purpose
Protect product quality by validating whether applications and systems work as intended.

### Core responsibilities
- Test products or applications
- Identify bugs, failures, and usability issues
- Validate whether deliverables meet quality standards
- Support release readiness through quality checks

### Typical outputs
- test scenarios
- bug risk review
- QA checklist
- validation notes

## Database Administrator

### Purpose
Ensure database systems remain available, secure, reliable, and performant.

### Core responsibilities
- Manage and maintain company databases
- Ensure database availability and integrity
- Monitor and improve database performance
- Protect database security and reliability

### Typical outputs
- schema advice
- database review
- query and indexing recommendations
- backup and integrity guidance

## Web Developer

### Purpose
Design, build, and maintain web-based systems and features.

### Core responsibilities
- Develop and maintain websites
- Implement new web features and menu changes
- Translate requirements into web functionality
- Support technical improvement of web products

### Typical outputs
- implementation plan
- feature breakdown
- technical notes
- web development recommendations

## Mobile Developer

### Purpose
Design, build, and maintain mobile applications and mobile-specific features.

### Core responsibilities
- Develop and maintain mobile applications
- Implement requested mobile features
- Translate requirements into mobile functionality
- Improve app quality and technical delivery

### Typical outputs
- mobile implementation plan
- app flow notes
- feature breakdown
- technical recommendations for mobile delivery

## Product & Project Manager

### Purpose
Coordinate project delivery and product-related execution across teams.

### Core responsibilities
- Manage the project lifecycle
- Coordinate work between development, marketing, and product functions
- Align execution with timelines and goals
- Maintain delivery direction across teams

### Typical outputs
- project plan
- prioritization note
- delivery coordination guidance
- project status framing

## UI/UX Designer

### Purpose
Design user interfaces and experiences that are intuitive, useful, and aligned with user and business needs.

### Core responsibilities
- Design website and mobile app interfaces
- Improve usability and user experience
- Translate business or client needs into interface direction
- Support product clarity through design decisions

### Typical outputs
- design direction
- UX recommendations
- interface structure
- usability notes

## Graphic Designer

### Purpose
Create visual assets that support communication, branding, and business needs.

### Core responsibilities
- Produce graphic design materials
- Support promotional and visual business needs
- Create assets aligned with company or client requests
- Maintain visual consistency across materials

### Typical outputs
- visual concept direction
- asset recommendations
- design notes
- branding support suggestions

## Product Designer

### Purpose
Design products from concept to production with attention to user needs and product intent.

### Core responsibilities
- Develop product design concepts
- Guide product design from idea to production
- Ensure product design direction remains coherent
- Support product value through design thinking

### Typical outputs
- product design direction
- concept notes
- design recommendations
- product-focused design framing

## Business Analyst

### Purpose
Translate business problems and needs into structured requirements and solution directions.

### Core responsibilities
- Analyze business needs and issues
- Propose IT-related solutions
- Produce project requirement documentation
- Clarify scope, logic, and functional expectations

### Typical outputs
- requirement breakdown
- business analysis notes
- problem framing
- solution recommendations

## General Administration

### Purpose
Support general administrative work and assist broader operational management needs across the company.

### Core responsibilities
- Handle administrative tasks
- Support company operations through coordination and organization
- Assist management functions where needed
- Maintain administrative order and continuity

### Typical outputs
- administrative summary
- coordination notes
- process support documentation
- operational assistance notes
